GPHQ is designed to make your everyday practice easier and more efficient.

Features

GPHQ’s features are designed to make your working day as a GP more efficient,allowing you extra time to devote to patient care.

VIW ALL

Specialist Directory

Find the right specialist for your patient quickly, first time, every time. Our carefully curated database has been personally compiled and checked by 4 Perth GPs. It is up to date and searchable by location, specialty and subspecialty. Information about billing and speed of access is included where possible.

My Notes

Ever written down a clinical pearl, treatment regime, or any information you want use again in the future? And lost the piece of paper? Jot them down in MyNotes and never lose them again. Access this feature on your smartphone or tablet at your next medical meeting and tap in the key points. Find that information quickly and easily when you need it.

Events Calendar

Browse local, state and national educational events and plan your CPD easily.

Classifieds

Advertise a position at your practice or search for a job. There is also a section for buying and selling medical equipment.

Meet the Specialist

Local WA specialists answer questions about common conundrums and new treatments. See who you are referring your patients to.

Links

A list of websites and links for patients and doctors, including clinical guidelines.

Forum

Discuss clinical cases, and anything else you like, anonymously or under your own name. The GPHQ forum is secure, private, and accessible to GPs only.
